My Research Interest

Management Information System

My research interest lies in exploring the integration of emerging technologies within Management Information Systems, aiming to optimize organizational processes and decision-making through innovative digital solutions.

Software Engineering

My research interest in Software Engineering focuses on advancing methodologies and tools to enhance software development practices, with a particular emphasis on improving efficiency, quality, and scalability in modern software systems.

Machine Learning

My research interest in Machine Learning centers on developing novel algorithms and techniques to address complex problems across various domains, with a focus on advancing both theoretical understanding and practical applications of artificial intelligence.

Artificial Intelligence

My research interest in Artificial Intelligence revolves around designing intelligent systems that can mimic human cognitive processes, with a focus on advancing the capabilities of machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision to solve real-world challenges.
